Senior Full Stack Engineer - Job Description

Company: TechStart Inc.
Location: Remote (US)
Type: Full-time

About the Role
We are looking for a Senior Full Stack Engineer to join our growing team. You will build and maintain our customer-facing web applications and internal tools.

Requirements
- 4+ years of experience with React and Node.js
- Strong proficiency in TypeScript
- Experience with PostgreSQL or similar relational databases
- Backend architecture and REST API design
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS preferred)
- BS in Computer Science or equivalent

Nice to Have
- Kubernetes or container orchestration experience
- Experience with Redis or caching systems
- Leadership or mentoring experience

Responsibilities
- Design and implement scalable web applications
- Build RESTful APIs and integrate with databases
- Collaborate with product and design teams
- Mentor junior developers

Benefits
- Competitive salary
- Health insurance
- Remote work
- 401(k) matching
